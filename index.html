College Questions Answered

About Us

We are two students from UVA focused on answering hard-hitting questions that young students want to now about. We bring you answers from real students that have gone or are going through the issues that you may face in college. We will have webinars on those issues that people are most interested in.

Some example issues we may tackle:

  • Educational
    • Should you apply to every college when you have a low GPA.
  • Financial
    • How to have fun at college on a budget.
  • Health
    • How to handle pregnancy and college simultaneously.
  • Social
    • How to handle peer pressure to drink in college.
